About Frontenac

Service You Can Count On

Frontenac Crystal Springs is located in the Town of Clayton along the St. Lawrence River in the 1000 Islands Region of NY State. Frontenac Spring water was first enjoyed by island millionaires in the early 1900's, and continues to be a beverage of choice a century later.

Bottled directly at the source, Frontenac Crystal Springs and Jay Sea Distributing currently provide home and office delivery of bottled spring water and coffee service.

Our team of dedicated employees distribute our products in 7 counties throughout Upstate and Central New York.


 Our Principles
 Frontenac Crystal Springs believes these principles are important to our success:

Quality Frontenac Crystal Springs strives on this. The quality of our products are the backbone of all we do. Without quality, nothing else can follow. Our source has been highly recognized as far as taste and purity.

Service With many years in the industry, we have a proven reputation for service in both products and delivery.

Experience Our current management team has a combined total of 52 years experience in the bottled water industry. We are a family owned and operated company that not only manages the company, but we are also personally involved in the finished product.

Testing We follow all New York State and Pennsylvania State Health Department requirements. We are required to perform tests on our water, which is then submitted to a certified independent laboratory. In addition to state mandated testing, we perform our own onsite testing daily.
